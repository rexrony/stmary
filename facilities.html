Resources & Facilities

St. Mary's school
St. Mary's school is situated in the heart of Hyderabad. In central location makes it easily accessible from all parts of the city.
The school provides a quality education at minimum cost.
It offers a very conductive learning environment with appropriate and well equipped classrooms, laboratories, Computer lab and library facilities.
LIBRARY:
The library of St. Mary's School includes extensive and excellent collection of approximately (5106) course and reference books on various subjects. In addition, it subscribes to academic and professional magazines and newspapers, story books in Urdu and English. Students are encouraged to use the library for research and their studies

It has a ground used for sports and other event and activities and a school stage for school Assembly and Dramas. The basement hall, is dedicated to Sister Philomena Harris's name, honoring her great contribution and unforgetable services to this instituation. All indoor activities take place in this hall.
CLASS ROOMS:
The classrooms are large and airy. They are well lit and ventilated and also well equipped with furniture.
THE SCHOOL GROUNDS
The school has two big grounds on which all kinds of sporting activities and other events are conducted.

It provides opportunity to individuals to enjoy the school activities. LABORATORIES:
There are four science laboratories for conducting practicals in the subjects of Chemistry, Physics, Biology and Computer. All the laboratories are large, well ventilated and well equipped.



Today computers are the demand of
the world and for this reason students are encouraged to use the computer and internet to gather information and prepare assignments.
