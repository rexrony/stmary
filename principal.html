Sr. Philomena Harris

It was through the untiring efforts of Sr. Philomena Harris that our School was finally denationalized in 1993. Sister then took over as Principal and put her gifts and talents at the service of the students and teachers. Sr. Philomena was passionate about education and about putting the school in order.
She was a strict disciplinarian and in no time the school was back to its original high standard of education. Sister became known as the "Builder Queen". She built a new wing with 18 classrooms and a stage which could be used for dramas and other activities. Sr. Philomena was a determined and dynamic person and we are now reaping the benefits of all her efforts. We will always remember her with admiration and gratitude.
