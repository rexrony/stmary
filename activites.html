Events and Activities

The students activities are organized by the management and the staff. Such innovative ideas as science exhibition, millad, debates, colour day, sports, dramas, singing, girl guides, summer camp elocution, junior sports, annual award day, graphic contest and computer exhibition.
That serve to broadens students' horizon. Emphases is also laid on responsible citizenship. Students council raises funds to aid those less fortunate or victims of natural disasters.

THE STUDENTS COUNCIL:
