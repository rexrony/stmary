School Conduct & Discipline


St. Mary's School expects that the behavior of the students within and outside will be in conformity with the highest standards of morality and discipline. Students will respect the rights the privileges of the members of the school at all times and will conduct themselves in a manner that upholds the reputation of the school. Students, who do not abide by the rules of the school will be subject to disciplinary procedure.



  Please, pay the Tuition Fee Bi-Monthly (Once in Two Months). April-May, June-July, August-September, October-November, December-January and February-March.

  Parents are requested to pay the fees in the Bank. Please do not give any cash in the fee office or to any person in the School Campus. ONCE DEPOSITED Fees are NOT REFUNDABLE.

  If two months fees are not paid in time your child will not be allowed to sit in her class and thereafter her name will be STRUCK OFF the register. After the due date a FINE will be charged.

   All students will have to leave the school premises within half an hour after the closure of the school there after the responsibility rests with the parents. In case of emergency only the parents should request a gate pass from the main office.

  Please assure that your child is present for the School Assembly. If late she will not be allowed to attend the classes.

  Students in Class 9th , 10th , 11th & 12th have to appear and pass in both of the School Exams in order to be enrolled for the Board Exams.

  Students should be neatly dressed and in the School Uniform. Otherwise, they will not be allowed to attend the classes.

  Any student absent from school for a month at a stretch without a note of valid excuse explaining her absence will be struck off the roll. The judgment about the validity of the excuse rests with the PRINCIPAL.

  Please bring your C.N.I.C. when you come to the school, as you will be asked to show it at the gate.

  Because of SECURITY REASONS and on the instructions of the Authorities concerned ENTRY into the SCHOOL CAMPUS is being controlled.

  TEACHER PARENTS MEETING on the LAST SATURDAY of each Month. (along with the child).

   School Phone #: is 0223-642611.

  Looking forward to your continued co-operation to run the institution smoothly.
